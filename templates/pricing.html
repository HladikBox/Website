Pricing FAQs

  • Do I need a credit card to sign up for a Free trial?

    No, you dont! We believe that payment comes after weve earned your business, so our Free Trial is really free, you dont have to put in your credit card details to try our product. Simply reach out to us, and well set you up.

  • What level of customer support do I get?

    All Contentstack customers receive complimentary 24/5 in-app support from our Customer Success team with average response times under 5 minutes. Our Customer Success team offers additional support-level options, including plans that provide 24/7 support, so you can rest assured that we have you covered no matter when your questions arise.

  • Do you have detailed documentation?

    Our team is committed to providing our customers with all the information and resources they need in order to be independent and productive. Our clear and comprehensive documentation is up-to-date and among the best in the industry.

  • Can I upgrade my plan as my business grows?

    Absolutely! You can sign up for what your business needs now and upgrade your plan as your business grows.

  • Which plan should I start with?

    We recommend the Business plan for teams or organizations with a single digital property (e.g., website), a limited number of localized sites, and is looking to move away from a Drupal or Wordpress CMS. The Enterprise plan is an excellent choice for businesses with complex needs, such as approval workflows, sites in additional languages, and multiple digital properties, and is looking for an alternative to a monolithic CMS, such as Adobe Experience Manager or Sitecore. If you need help choosing a plan, our team is happy to help accomodate and assist you in your decision making.

  • What kind of security does your system have?

    Contentstack takes data security very seriously. We provide a multi-step security system with SOC 2 Type 2, VAPT, and ISO 27001 compliance so you not only feel safe, you can see how secure your data is, anytime. Learn more on our Trust page.
